QuizKlip Instructions:

QuizKlip is a word list builder and Quizlet helper for language learners. Build word lists for later study as you browse the web in your target foreign language. See an unfamiliar word in an article? Activate QuizKlip to "clip" the word, find dictionary definitions, and add them to your study list. Export the list at any time, or automatically create a Quizlet set for flashcard-style learning.

Step 1: Setup

A. Configure Language and Hotkey Settings

From the icon in the top-right corner of Chrome, select language settings and hotkey combination to activate translation.

Step 2: Using QuizKlip

A. Activate Translation

As you browse the web, use your selected hotkeys to activate translation on unfamiliar words.

(From Wikipedia)

B. Add Terms

Check the box next to each term you wish to add to your terms list.

C. Keep It Up!

Continue browsing and clipping additional terms.

Step 3: Use Terms List

A. View Terms

To view you clipped terms, click on the icon in the top-right corner of Chrome.

B. Create Study Sets

From the icon in the top-right corner of Chrome, Press "Create Quizlet Set" to automatically post your terms list to your Quizlet account as a new study set.
You must have at least two terms to create a Quizlet set.
You may also press "Copy to Clipboard" to copy and paste terms for your own list.

**Note** You will need to have a Quizlet account already to create a set. If you don't have an account, sign up for free here.
