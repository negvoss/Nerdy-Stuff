QuizKlip Privacy Policy

Updated 2018-04-25

By using QuizKlip, you consent to this Privacy Policy.

What information does QuizKlip collect about me?

QuizKlip does not collect any personally identifiable information.

QuizKlip does make use of a third-party analytics provider (“TPAP”), specifically Google Analytics, to track the number, demographics, and activity of users on QuizKlip. The TPAP may use information stored by cookies to, among other things, record your city and country, demographic profile, and time of use of QuizKlip. These data do not contain any personally identifiable information and are only used to help QuizKlip monitor user demographics.

Does QuizKlip have access to my username and password on other sites?

No, QuizKlip does not have access to your username and password on any sites.

Upon your request, QuizKlip may access your account on Quizlet, a third-party flashcard-style learning website, if you explicitly provide authorization via Quizlet for it to do so. This authorization is provided via the Quizlet website and will bear a message indicating that authorization is being provided to QuizKlip. This authorization is temporary and must be granted, by you, each time you request QuizKlip to post data to your Quizlet account. QuizKlip uses this authorization only to post your requested data to your Quizlet account via the Quizlet Application Programming Interface (“API”). QuizKlip never has access to your username or password.

Does QuizKlip know what websites I visit?

No, QuizKlip does not track your web activity.

What data does QuizKlip store?

QuizKlip stores your word list that you generate while using QuizKlip, as well as QuizKlip configuration settings. These data are stored on your local machine. Upon your request and authorization via Quizlet, QuizKlip may also transmit your word list to the third-party Quizlet website using your Quizlet account.

What does QuizKlip do with my search terms?

QuizKlip transmits your search terms to third-party online dictionary websites, such as wordreference.com and morfix.co.il, to receive definitions.

Does QuizKlip share any of my information?

No personally identifiable information is collected or shared by QuizKlip. However, QuizKlip may share summary demographic information about the QuizKlip user base, such as total number and geographic distribution of users.

Can this Privacy Policy be revised?

QuizKlip may from time to time update or revise this Privacy Policy. If QuizKlip updates or revises this Privacy Policy, the new policy will be posted on this webpage.

Your use of QuizKlip following any such update or revision constitutes your consent to the Privacy Policy as updated or revised.
