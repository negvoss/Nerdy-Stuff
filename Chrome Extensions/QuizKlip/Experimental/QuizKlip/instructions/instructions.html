QuizKlip Instructions:

QuizKlip is a word list builder and Quizlet helper for language learners. Build word lists for later study as you browse the web in your target foreign language. See an unfamiliar word in an article? Activate QuizKlip to "clip" the word, find dictionary definitions, and add them to your study list. Export the list to Quizlet for flashcard-style learning.

Step 1: Configure Language and Hotkey Settings

From in the top-right corner of Chrome, select language settings and hotkey combination to activate translation.

Step 2: Using QuizKlip

A. Activate Translation

As you browse the web, use your selected hotkeys to activate translation on unfamiliar words.

You may also select the word, right-click on it, and select "Find Translations" from the menu.

B. Add Terms

Check the box next to each term you wish to add to your terms list, then press "Add Selected Terms"

C. Keep It Up!

Continue browsing and clipping additional terms.

Step 3: Use Terms List

A. View Terms

To view your clipped terms, click in the top-right corner of Chrome.

B. Create Study Sets

From in the top-right corner of Chrome, delete terms, automatically copy terms to the clipboard, or automatically post terms to your Quizlet account as a new study set.

**Note** You will need to have a Quizlet account already to create a set. If you don't have an account, sign up for free.
